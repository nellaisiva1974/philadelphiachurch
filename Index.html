Welcome to the Philadelphia Fellowship of India

The Philadelphia Fellowship is a place where you will find keys from the word of God to unlock the riches which has been reserved for you and also to find the keys to lock and seal the blessings so that the enemy does not steal or snatch away the promised privileges which is meant for the body of Christ.

This fellowship was founded by Rev. Bhasker Dawson in the year 1956. His charisma and passion towards God and His Word has brought so many souls into the kingdom of God. He has been an inspiration to many Christian leaders and has raised many leaders who are now serving the Lord in many parts of the world. He is a gifted songwriter, musician, Bible teacher and preacher. Having built and established the leadership and congregation on the sure foundation of Jesus Christ and after serving the lord for over 40 years, he went home to be with the Lord in 1994.

After Rev. Bhasker Dawson, his son Pr. Charles Dawson took on the mantle to fulfilling the mandate that God has given to this fellowship. Since 1994 he has been leading this flock in the foundation of the Word without turning to the left or right as commanded to Joshua. The Philadelphia Fellowship seeks to build, encourage and inspire people with the gospel of Jesus Christ to equip every believer to become a preacher and a teacher of the word.  Philadelphia Fellowship is a place one comes in to be equipped to go and PREACH REACH EACH with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
